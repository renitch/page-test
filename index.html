The Arkansas Black Mayors Association (ABMA) is a coalition of forward-thinking leaders dedicated  to advancing
the concerns of  Black mayors and communities across Arkansas. Our goal is to foster economic growth, 
revitalize neighborhoods,  and engage in political advocacy to improve the well-being 
of residents in Black-majority municipalities.

Helena / West Helena
celebrates next phase of
watershed project

The Arkansas Black Mayors Association, in
partnership with Mayor Joseph Whitfield of
Helena-West Helena and Mayor Lisa Hicks-Gilbert
of Elaine, recently hosted a joint press conference
to announce the commencement of the design
phase for the ABMA Watershed Project for their
respective cities.
